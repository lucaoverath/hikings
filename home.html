Inca Trail

One of the most popular hikes in the world, the Inca Trail takes you through 26 miles of grueling inclines and declines. You get a chance to explore ruins, jungle and beautiful mountain scenery, and of course, end the hike at Machu Picchu.

Walk on the Wildside

This is one of my favorite hiking places in the area. It's a bit off the beaten path which is a good thing. The hike isn't that long but it is steep and there really isn't a great trail so be careful. Going behind the falls is awesome. If you go the other direction make sure you climb down the embankment to the creek bed. There is another spectacular waterfall that most don't know about. I am going to kije the creek all the way the Skaneateles lake this year, I can't wait! A MUST SEE!
